Burdock and Carrot Kinpira

Description

Sauté thinly sliced burdock and carrot in sesame oil, add seasoning, and stir-fry until the moisture evaporates. That's it! You'll have delicious kinpira gobo ready to enjoy

Ingredients

  • 1 burdock root
  • 1/2 carrot
  • 1 red chili pepper
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • (A) 2 tablespoons sugar
  • (A) 1 tablespoon sake (Japanese rice wine)
  • (A) 1 tablespoon mirin (Japanese sweet rice wine)
  • (A) 2.5 tablespoons soy sauce
  • (A) 3 tablespoons water

Steps

  1. Peel the burdock root and carrot, then cut them into thin strips.
  2. Remove the seeds from the red chili pepper and thinly slice it.
  3. Heat sesame oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Add the burdock root and carrot strips, along with the red chili pepper, and sauté until slightly tender.
  5. In a bowl, mix together the ingredients listed under (A): sugar, sake, mirin, soy sauce, and water.
  6. Pour the (A) mixture into the pan with the vegetables.
  7. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the liquid has evaporated and the vegetables are tender and glazed.
  8. Serve your delicious kinpira gobo hot or cold as a side dish or topping. Enjoy!
