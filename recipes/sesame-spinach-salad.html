Sesame Spinach Salad

Description

This dish is highly recommended for times when you're in need of a simple side dish or when you feel like something is missing from your meal. With just one ingredient and seasoning, it's easy to prepare.

The subtly sweet taste combined with the aromatic flavor of ground sesame seeds blends perfectly with soy sauce, stimulating the appetite.

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch of spinach
  • Salt, to taste
  • 15g ground sesame seeds
  • 2 teaspoons of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce

Steps

  1. Quickly blanch the spinach in boiling water with a pinch of salt. Once it turns bright green, transfer it to cold water, then squeeze out excess water.
  2. Cut the spinach into 3cm widths.
  3. Prepare the dressing by mixing ground sesame seeds, sugar, and soy sauce.
  4. Combine step 2 with the dressing, and it's ready to serve.
